# Incident Response FAQ

## Q: How is a security incident detected?
A: Incidents are detected using alerting from SIEM, anomaly detection, and on-call monitoring.

## Q: What is the first step after confirmation?
A: Containment is the first priority. Access keys are rotated and impacted systems are isolated.

## Q: Who gets notified?
A: The on-call security engineer, incident commander, and affected stakeholders are notified immediately.

## Q: Is there a post-incident review?
A: Yes. A blameless postmortem is documented with root cause and preventive actions.

## Q: Are customers notified?
A: Customer notification follows legal and contractual obligations after validated impact assessment.
