# Data Theft Prevention FAQ

## Q: What are common data exfiltration risks in GenAI?
A: Prompt injection, overly broad retrieval, unrestricted tool access, and unsafe output handling.

## Q: How do we prevent prompt injection attacks?
A: Apply input classification, deny unsafe tool instructions, and separate system policy from user content.

## Q: How can we reduce accidental leakage?
A: Enforce least privilege, data classification tags, output filtering, and DLP scanning.

## Q: What should we do if theft is suspected?
A: Contain access, rotate credentials, preserve logs, notify incident response, and begin forensic analysis.

## Q: How do we prove compliance?
A: Maintain audit trails, policy enforcement logs, periodic access reviews, and incident records.
