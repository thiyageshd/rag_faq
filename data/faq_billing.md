# Billing FAQ

## Q: What payment methods do we support?
A: We support Visa, Mastercard, and American Express. Annual enterprise contracts can be paid by bank transfer.

## Q: When is an invoice generated?
A: Invoices are generated on the first day of each billing cycle and emailed to billing contacts.

## Q: Do you offer refunds?
A: Monthly plans are refundable within 14 days of the first payment. Usage charges are non-refundable.

## Q: Can I change from monthly to annual billing?
A: Yes. Plan changes are applied at the next billing cycle unless a custom contract is signed.

## Q: What happens if payment fails?
A: We retry payment up to 3 times over 7 days. Account access is restricted after repeated failures.
